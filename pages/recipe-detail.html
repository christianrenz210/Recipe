Chicken Adobo

Chef Maria Santos Published on June 17, 2026
45 mins
6 Servings
Easy
4.9 (256 reviews)

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Marinate the chicken

In a large bowl, combine chicken thighs with soy sauce, crushed garlic, and crushed peppercorns. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to absorb the flavors.

2
Sear the chicken

Heat cooking oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Remove chicken from the marinade (reserve the liquid) and sear each piece until golden brown on both sides.

3
Simmer in adobo sauce

Pour the reserved marinade, vinegar, bay leaves, and water into the pot. Do not stir. Bring to a boil without stirring to cook out the vinegar's sharpness.

4
Slow cook until tender

Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until the chicken is tender and the sauce has reduced to half. Stir occasionally.

5
Serve and enjoy

Transfer to a serving plate and pour the remaining sauce on top. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy your authentic Filipino Chicken Adobo!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar (cane or white)
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Steamed rice for serving
Chef's Tips
  • Do not stir after adding vinegar — let it boil first to remove the raw taste.
  • Use cane vinegar (sukang Iloko) for authentic flavor.
  • Add a pinch of sugar to balance the sourness and saltiness.
