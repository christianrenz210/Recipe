Our Story

RecipeShare was born in 2024 from a simple idea: every Filipino family has a recipe worth sharing. What started as a small blog between friends quickly grew into a vibrant community of food lovers, home cooks, and professional chefs celebrating Filipino cuisine.

We believe that food brings people together. Whether you're a seasoned chef or just learning to cook adobo, RecipeShare is your home for discovering new Filipino flavors, sharing your family recipes, and connecting with fellow food enthusiasts.

Today, we're proud to host thousands of Filipino recipes spanning every province and cuisine, and our community continues to grow every day.
